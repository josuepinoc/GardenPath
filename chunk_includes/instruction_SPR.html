Sentence reading task

In this task, you will read some sentences and answer a question about the sentences that you read.

In each trial, a sentence will be presented word-by-word. You will press the spacebar to make each word appear. You should read each sentence at a normal pace.

Once you're done reading the sentence, press the spacebar to see the question. Each question will have two possible answers: "Yes" and "No". To respond, use the mouse to click on your answer, or press the number on the keyboard that corresponds to your answer (1 or 2). Once you have answered the question, press the spacebar to see the next sentence.

Before the experiment begins, you will go through 3 practice trials to familiarize yourself with the procedure. During the practice session, please adjust the font size on your screen for comfortable viewing. You can make the font bigger by pressing CTRL + (command + on Mac) on the keyboard. You can make the font smaller by pressing CTRL - (command - on Mac).

When you're ready, click below to continue.
